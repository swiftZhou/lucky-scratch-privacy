Frequently asked questions

Is Lucky Scratch real-money gambling?

No. Lucky Scratch uses virtual coins only. Coins and prizes have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for real money or physical items. The App is for entertainment only.

How do I get more coins?

Win coins by scratching tickets, or claim your daily free Classic Match ticket from the home screen (one per calendar day). Version 1.0 does not include real-money purchases.

How do I turn off sound or vibration?

Open Settings from the home screen (gear icon). Toggle Sound Effects and Haptic Feedback on or off.

Will my progress be saved?

Yes. Your coin balance, statistics, and preferences are saved locally on your device. Uninstalling the App will remove this data. We do not offer cloud backup in version 1.0.

Does the App collect my personal data?

Version 1.0 does not require an account and does not upload gameplay data to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I report a bug?

Email zhouhaigame@outlook.com with steps to reproduce the issue and, if possible, a screen recording.
